Design Principle Page

Repetition

Source: NINTENDO

Repetition is found in this website with the colors that are used. The bright red color is repeated multiple times throughout the website. There is a smooth flow of content because the red color brings a sense of cohesive relationship between the other elements.

Fitt's Law

Source: DISNEY PLUS

The first element that grabs our attention is the big blue button that says, "Get the Disney Bundle". Because of it's big size, it is easier for users to feel drawn to it and it increases the usability of it as well.

White Space

Source: LG ELECTRONICS

Negative space is found in this website. Users can see "empty" portions of space in the web page which creates white space. This allows the website to have a very clean and simple layout and this makes it very easy for users to read through the contents. It allows the website to communicate a clear message towards the audience.
