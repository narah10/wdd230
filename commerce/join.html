Member Benefits

Gold ($1000)

  • Featured once annually on Chamber Facebook page, boosted to 100,000+ viewers in the surrounding area
  • Annual meeting with Executive Director and Board Officer
  • An annual feature in Chamber's e-newsletter
  • Company logo on Roof Top Drop bags and inside New Resident Welcome bags
  • Company logo featured in an annual ad in BizWest
  • First crack at sponsorships for Chamber’s signature events

Silver ($400)

  • Featured once annually on Chamber Facebook page, boosted to 26,000+ viewers in surrounding area
  • Free standard booth at Town of Superior events (July 4th & Chili Fest)
  • Up to two free one-time use email list or mailing labels for your marketing purposes
  • Two free tickets to the Annual Awards event
  • Company logo featured in an annual ad in BizWest

Bronze ($200)

  • Enhanced listing on website directory including logo and second category
  • One-time use email list or mailing labels for your marketing purposes
  • List “Job Openings” on Chamber website
  • Free inclusion of your marketing materials in monthly New Resident Welcome Bags

Non-Profit ($100)

  • Business name and description, website, and social media links on our website directory
  • Post your events on the Chamber's online Calendar of Events
  • Invitation to a new member event in your first year
  • Referrals from Chamber staff and volunteers

The West Seattle Chamber of Commerce is ready to work with you to improve the lives of the community. Your Growth is our Focus and we will join you in your journey of development.
