Target Audience

The target audience for this website is for individuals who are 12 and above. Both locals and tourists are targeted as there will be different kinds of events and acitivities that they can all participate in. Anybody who wants to find out more about the weather in the area and wants to be updated on different kinds of news and events are targets for this website

Persona 1

Riley Marshall

Riley is 24 years old and is a Female. She is originally from Redmond, Washington and goes to the University of Washington. Riley has one year left to graduate with her Bachelor's in Chemistry. One of her goals before graduating is to visit all states and she is currently touring around the area. Riley has been a photographer since her senior year of high school and she is excited to take all the beautiful pictures from her trips in the United States.

Scenarios

  1. She wants to know if it will be raining that day because she is hoping to take a photo of a bird's nest she had found the day before.
  2. She wants to know what the locals like to do on a weekend. She misses social life and is looking to interact with new people
  3. She wants to go out to eat that afternoon at a nice outdoor restaurant. She would like to know what the weather would be like for lunch time
